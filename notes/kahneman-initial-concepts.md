## 暫定可轉化概念

### 1. System 1 提供快速、聯想式反應；System 2 負責較慢且需要努力的分析。

- ledger: C-001, C-002, C-003
- B4-C-001 (Part I Ch.1): "System 1 operates automatically and quickly, with little or no effort and no sense of voluntary control. System 2 allocates attention to the effortful mental activities that demand it, including complex computations."
- B4.2-C-001 (第1章): "系统1的运行是无意识且快速的，不怎么费脑力...系统2将注意力转移到需要费脑力的大脑活动上来。"
- B4.1-C-001 (I): "the fast and intuitive System 1, and the slow and effortful System 2."
- management_inference: 管理者在重大決策中不能僅依賴 System 1 的快速直覺，需刻意啟動 System 2 進行分析。但 System 2 懶惰且資源有限（C-004），因此需要結構化流程（如檢查表、逐步訪談）來強制 System 2 介入。

### 2. 重大管理決策不能只依賴直覺熟悉感或單一故事。

- ledger: C-005, C-006, C-007, C-008
- B4-C-017 (Part I Ch.7): "WYSIATI... System 1 is radically insensitive to both the quality and the quantity of the information."
- B4-C-018 (Part I Ch.7): "The confidence that individuals have in their beliefs depends mostly on the quality of the story they can tell about what they see, even if they see little."
- B4.2-C-009 (第7章): "系统1基本上对于引起印象和直觉的信息的质量和数量都不敏感。"
- B4-C-043 (Part I Ch.7): "They made that big decision on the basis of a good report from one consultant. WYSIATI... They did not seem to realize how little information they had."
- management_inference: 管理者常以"故事連貫性"而非"證據品質"來判斷信心。決策審查需檢查：故事背後有多少證據？是否有被忽略的資訊？

### 3. anchoring、availability、framing、overconfidence 可能改變判斷。

- ledger: C-011~C-014 (anchoring), C-015~C-017 (availability), C-048~C-051 (framing), C-023~C-026 (overconfidence)
- B4.2-C-012 (第11章): "这一效应是实验心理学中最可靠也最稳健的结果。"
- B4.2-C-014 (第11章): "专业人士...都受到了锚定效应的影响……两者的唯一区别是，学生承认...而专业人士则否认这一点。"
- B4-C-024 (Introduction): "We called this reliance on the ease of memory search the availability heuristic."
- B4.2-C-018 (第12章): "这位执行总裁连续多次成功，因此失败不会轻易在她的脑海中出现。可得性偏见使得她过于自信。"
- B4-C-019 (Part I Ch.7): "'the odds of survival one month after surgery are 90%' is more reassuring than... 'mortality within one month of surgery is 10%.'"
- B4.2-C-060 (第34章): "医生和对医学涉猎不深的人一样，也容易受到框架效应的影响。"
- B4.1-C-011: "A high level of confidence in someone making forecasts is never a reliable measure of accuracy."
- management_inference: 管理決策審查必須逐一檢查這四種偏誤。錨定效應即使影響專家也不被承認；可得性偏誤使連續成功的 CEO 過度自信；框架效應連醫生都無法免疫；過度自信使預測者的高信心不成為準確性指標。

### 4. outside view、base rate 與替代方案可作為決策審查的補強。

- ledger: C-032~C-040
- B4-C-041 (Part II Ch.14): "When the evidence is weak, one should stick with the base rates."
- B4-C-042 (Part II Ch.14): "This start-up looks as if it could not fail, but the base rate of success in the industry is extremely low. How do we know this case is different?"
- B4.2-C-030 (第23章): "在与内部意见的竞争中，外部意见丝毫没有取胜的机会。"
- B4.2-C-032 (第23章): "在预测时使用相似团队的分布信息被称为采纳"外部意见"，它是避免规划谬误的有效方法。"
- B4-C-031 (Part II Ch.18): "Anchor your judgment of the probability of an outcome on a plausible base rate. Question the diagnosticity of your evidence."
- management_inference: 決策審查的 Convergent phase 須強制引入外部視角：該產業/專案的基礎成功率是多少？此案與參考類別有何不同？證據薄弱時應堅持基礎比率。

### 5. 小規模試點可把不確定性轉化為可觀察的結果，但仍需預先定義指標與停止條件。

- ledger: C-076, C-077, C-078, C-082, C-083
- B4.1-C-028 (A Final Word): "organizations make better decisions (they move more slowly and employ useful checklists)... By simply slowing down before making important decisions and seeking assistance from System 2, one can broaden the frame for a problem, gather pertinent data, arrive at a decision, reflect upon it, and review it for accuracy."
- B4.2-C-066 (终章): "机构可以制定和有效使用检查表，还可以深入开展，比如参考类别的预测以及"事前验尸"练习。"
- B4.2-C-039 (第24章): "设想我们在一年后的今天已经实施了现有计划，但结果惨败。请用5~10分钟简短写下这次惨败的缘由。"
- B4.1-C-020 (II): "Kahneman says a better way to alleviate regret is to explicitly anticipate it. If the outcome is bad, remembering that one considered the possibility of regret beforehand will decrease its effect."
- B4-C-005 (Part I Ch.1): "The best we can do is a compromise: learn to recognize situations in which mistakes are likely and try harder to avoid significant mistakes when the stakes are high."
- management_inference: 試點設計應結合事前驗屍（預想失敗原因）、預先遺憾預期、以及明確的 go/revise/stop 門檻。組織比個人更適合做規避錯誤的決策，因為人多思慢、可按規則行事。
